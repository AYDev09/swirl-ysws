Ice Cream Flavours

Notes on different ice cream flavours

Vanilla

  • Most common flavour
  • Made from vanilla beans
  • Tastes sweet and creamy

Chocolate

  • Made with cocoa powder
  • Rich and sweet taste
  • Second most popular flavour

Strawberry

  • Contains real strawberries
  • Light pink color
  • Fruity and refreshing

Mint Chocolate Chip

  • Mint flavored base
  • Has chocolate chips mixed in
  • Tastes cool and refreshing

Cookies and Cream

  • Vanilla with cookie pieces
  • Usually uses Oreo cookies
  • Crunchy texture

Salted Caramel

  • Sweet caramel with salt
  • Has caramel swirls
  • Popular modern flavour

Pistachio

  • Made with pistachio nuts
  • Light green color
  • Nutty flavor

Mango

  • Made from mango fruit
  • Tropical and sweet
  • Bright orange color
